About

My name is Mike. I'm a 17 year old aspiring Web Designer from England. I'm currently studying web design and multimedia with the goal to become a highly experienced web designer. Throughout my two years at college I have developed skills in Graphic Design, Animation, 3D Modeling and web design. You can find examples of my work on the Portfolio page, and my CV is available on request.

Some of my hobbies include: Playing tean based games on my PC, I find team based games a challenge and I really enjoy communicating and playing with other people. I also enjoy programming in my spare time as it offers a sense of accomplishment, and unique problem solving opportunities. Oh, and I also have an unhealthy obsession to TV Shows and Films.
